Integrated interpretation using forecast, SPI, exceedance probability, probability terciles, risk, and skill layers for the selected polygon or lead area.
How to interpret the alert levels
The labels combine rainfall anomaly, SPI dryness/wetness, below-normal probability, heavy-rain probability, risk class, and model skill. They are intended for early warning, planning, and coordination.
●
DRY = high risk
Clear dry signal or water-stress condition. Impacts are likely. Take early action now.
!
WATCH = moderate risk
Uncertain or emerging signal. Monitor closely, prepare resources, and update plans.
◎
LOW–MODERATE = low risk
Near-normal or weak signal. Continue routine monitoring and stay ready for changes.
